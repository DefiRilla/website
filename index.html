Motivation

I started working full time on this personal project three and a half years ago after getting badly liquidated for the third time.


I did not notice for weeks, nor that my remaining crypto was returned to me converted into stable coins.


Unfortunately, when I did notice the crypto market had pumped considerably, and I was only able to convert the returned stable coins into a tiny fraction of my original crypto.


Like almost all DeFi sites, they had no kind of monitoring or alerting system I could have set up.


The final straw was when Aave removed their health notification system - I knew I had to find a comprehensive DeFi monitoring solution.


I tried every existing "monitoring" and "alert" system but none provided the health factor alerts I required across ALL DeFi yield protocols.


So I set out on a personal project to build realtime notification alerts for myself and my friends.


It turned out to be much more complicated than I anticipated to autodetect and support ALL DeFi sites across ALL EVM chains and provide realtime alerts on initially just their health factors.


But I finally managed it.


It was then relatively trivial to add a whole range of different alerts which save us time and effort including calculating real reward, borrow & supply APRs, when our DeFi limit orders hit and alerts when our LPs are about to enter or range - all across ALL DeFi sites on ALL EVM blockchains.


When describing the platform to my non crypto mentors, as there is nothing like it, they recommend I seek feedback from other crypto DeFi experts if it also adds value to them and is worth developing into a Startup to help others already in, or those who want to start using DeFi?


And here we are today.


I am still working on it full time to keep the numerous servers running, resolve bugs and implement requested features.


I appreciate you being early and your time and expertise providing feedback to improve this novel service making DeFi easier, safer and more rewarding.
